Terms and use

I assumes of use for a website or app. A terms of use agreement is a legal document that outlines the rules and regulations that users must follow when accessing a website or app. It also specifies the rights and responsibilities of the website or app owner. The terms of use agreement is a contract between the user and the website or app owner. It is not required by law, but it is in the best interest of the website or app owner to have one.


You can find a free terms of use template and a guide on how to create one for your website or app at Termly1. The template covers the most common clauses in a terms of use agreement, including limitations of liability, property rights, and dispute resolution. It also provides tips for having a good terms of use policy.
